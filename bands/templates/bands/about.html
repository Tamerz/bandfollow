Why we built this website?

Our story begins in Doha, Qatar. We are expat professionals who love music and tend to miss live music events of our favorite artists, venues and events.

What services do we provide?

BandFollow is a free service offering notification of live music events near you via a subscription model. Mobile-friendly and simple user interface, this site provides the user the ability to add artists, venues and events manually. Once it is approved by an admin, any BandFollow member can subscribe for notification.

Who are we?

We are a group of Northwestern School of Professional Studies (SPS) graduate students working to earn more than an "A" on our Capstone project.
