ABOUT THE PROJECT

Key features of our project

Dedication

BandFollow is a free service dedicated provide notification of live music events near you via a subscription model.

Passion

Our story begins in Doha, Qatar. We are expat professionals who love music and tend to miss live music events of our favorite artists, genres and venues.

Masters

We are a group of Northwestern School of Professional Studies (SPS) graduate students working to earn more than an "A" on our Capstone project.

Community Driven

Mobile-friendly and simple user interface, this site provides the user the ability to add artists, venues and events manually. Once it is approved by an admin, any BandFollow member can subscribe for notification.
